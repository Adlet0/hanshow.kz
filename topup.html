Our Story

Founded in 2012, Hanshow ESL has been at the forefront of electronic shelf label technology, revolutionizing how retailers manage pricing and inventory across the globe.

With over a decade of innovation and expertise, we've helped thousands of retailers streamline their operations, reduce costs, and improve accuracy through our cutting-edge smart pricing solutions.

Our commitment to excellence and continuous innovation has made us the trusted partner for retailers worldwide, from small boutiques to large supermarket chains.

  • Исключает необходимость вручную обновлять ценники
  • Исключает несоответствие между ценником и ценой на кассе
  • Централизованное удалённое управление ценами во всей сети магазинов (через SaaS)
  • Снижает затраты на рабочую силу, позволяя персоналу сосредоточиться на обслуживании клиентов
  • Улучшает внешний вид магазина
  • Ускоряет сбор заказов
  • Больше информации о товаре для покупателей
  • Сокращает время обнаружения отсутствующих товаров и их пополнения

Our Services

Complete support from consultation to maintenance

🔍

Consultation & Planning

Our experts analyze your retail environment and design the optimal ESL solution tailored to your specific needs. We conduct thorough site surveys, assess your current systems, and create detailed implementation plans that ensure seamless integration with your existing infrastructure.

⚙️

Installation & Setup

Professional installation services ensure your ESL system is deployed correctly and efficiently. Our certified technicians handle everything from hardware mounting to network configuration, ensuring minimal disruption to your daily operations while maximizing system performance.

🔗

System Integration

Seamless integration with your existing POS systems, inventory management, and ERP solutions. We provide custom APIs and middleware solutions that enable real-time data synchronization and automated price updates across all your retail channels.

📚

Training & Support

Comprehensive training programs for your staff to maximize the benefits of your ESL system. We provide hands-on training, detailed documentation, and ongoing support to ensure your team can effectively manage and maintain the system.

🛠️

Maintenance & Updates

Proactive maintenance services and regular system updates keep your ESL solution running at peak performance. Our remote monitoring capabilities allow us to identify and resolve issues before they impact your operations, ensuring maximum uptime.

📊

Analytics & Reporting

Advanced analytics and reporting tools provide valuable insights into your pricing strategies, customer behavior, and operational efficiency. Generate custom reports, track performance metrics, and make data-driven decisions to optimize your retail operations.
